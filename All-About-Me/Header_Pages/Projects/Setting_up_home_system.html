Things I have accomplished in my own home network

I have continuously upgraded and improved my home network through things that I have learned through education and experiences. Through this, I have found that my home network resembles more of a corporate mesh network than it does a personal private network.

» I created my own custom length cat 6 cables throughout the house.

» Created a mesh network with multiple Asus routers.

» Created my own custom subnet so that all devices are not on the customary IP addresses.

» Made separate networks that are firewall from each other for each set of devices on my network.

» Hide my SS id.

» Incorporated a stringent VPN for all traffic that goes through my network.

» Enforcing a MAC filtering for all devices on my network.

» Limiting the number of devices allowed all my network.

» Enforcing parental controls for certain devices on my network.

I have enacted each of the security protocols to keep my network and most importantly my family safe.
