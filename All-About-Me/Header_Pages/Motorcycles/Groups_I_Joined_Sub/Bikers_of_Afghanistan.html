MISSION STATEMENT: B.O.A.’s mission is to honor and empower America’s wounded warriors by joining together bikers and enthusiasts alike who have served or are serving or who support America’s military and civilians in the Global War on Terror.

B.O.A. Bikers of Afghanistan was organized on March 20th 2010 with in the confines of Camp Clark in the Khost Province,Afghanistan.The organization was created by civilian military employees and Soldiers alike with a common interest in motorcycles but perhaps greater still,with the purpose of being a charitable organization fronting the expressed goal of benefiting injured soldiers via the Wounded Warriors Charitable Organization.

B.O.A. Officers were voted into office during the second meeting of the organization on 01 April 2010 ,with the original 13 charter members plus one additional member present,for the proceedings. The BOA organization is operational under the Roberts Rules of Order and extends the privilege of charter membership to all whom desire to be part of this organization ,up to 20 May 2010
